There is evidence to suggest climate change is affecting our marine environments and the species within it. If you have just spotted a new or unusual species in your favourite waterhole you are not alone. Join Redmap in helping to record and track this pattern of species movement.

How can you help?

    Tell your friends

  • Log your sightings

    Scientists use your observations to help them understand what changes are occurring in the marine environment.

    This way you are always up to date with how things are changing.
